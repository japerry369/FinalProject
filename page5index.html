Mission Statement

Clean and Sober Residence strives to be the model of excellence and is dedicated to providing a clean, safe and educational environment to support the recovering addict. We achieve this goal by providing the highest quality services tailored to the individual needs of our clients that promotes the highest standard of care with integrity and quality.

Each year, Clean and Sober staff members attend continuing education classes given by the Behavioral Health Special Initiative (BHSI) to ensure our staff is keeps up with cutting edge treatment and new programs that residents may qualify to receive services.

Clean and Service Residence is part of the National Alliance of Recovery Residences or NARR and the Pennsylvania Alliance of Recovery Residences and adhere to the highest standards in treatment and recovery as prescribed by the national and local level.

Our Program

Our transitional housing program provides supportive housing along with drug and alcohol treatment for residents. We also provide supportive services that will enable you to live more independently. Our residents are sober and presently in detox, and drug treatment services. Addiction rehabilitation is a big part of program. We will provide you with the resources you need to re-enter society.

Each year we celebrate the Memorial Day Holiday with our big barbeque for family and friends. We respect all religions at Clean and Sober and have the Thanksgiving Feast, Christmas Feast, Ramadam Feast and the Yom Kippur Meal.
