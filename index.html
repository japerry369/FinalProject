    People Helping People

    Promotes Empowerment Through Education and Employment

    Clean and Sober Residence subscribes to the belief that there is empowerment through treatment, education and employment. Our treatment model is tailored to the individual and uses a cognitive behavioral approach that focuses on preventive measures through changing behavior and poor choices. Our educational model is based on a social cognitive theory that subscribes to the theory that behavioral change is determined by environmental, personal, and behavioral elements.

    Our Program

    Assisting the recovering addict in reclaiming his life through education and employment while recovering in a safe, clean and warm residential environment. We provide a peaceful environment and assist the resident in his recovery. Our facility offers a computer room, a fully equipped commercial kitchen and a resident telephone. We have partnerships with various treatment programs.

    Clean and Sober residence assist those drug-dependent residents, attempting to successfully assimilate into society, by providing short term recovery housing, life skills, training and financial literacy which include money management, credit and financial planning.

    Our facility has been recently updated. Our residential quarters have cable television. We provide assistance for job training and job placement. We ensure the family will be involved to make the transition easier for the resident.

    We will provide you with GED classes, training for employment, assistance with resumes and various assistance the resident may be qualified to receive.
